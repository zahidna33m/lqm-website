Class Regulations

Online Class Regulations

You must have a Zoom account to register and attend online classes. If you are new to Zoom, sign up for a free Zoom account first before proceeding with registration.

  1. Age Requirement: Students must be 15 years or older.
  2. Classroom Etiquette:
    • Use First and Last name when joining the class.
    • Keep microphones muted unless permitted (asking questions, answering on your turn, etc.).
    • Keep video off unless permitted by the teacher.
    • Do not address the class without the teacher's permission.
    • Do not ask irrelevant questions or engage in discussions, arguments, or cause disruptions.
    • Do not record the class.
  3. Conduct:
    • No announcements of events or programs without the permission of the teacher or LQM Organization.
    • No selling, promoting, or conducting business.
    • No activities which may be deemed a nuisance or are of an unlawful nature during or outside the class that may affect the teacher or organization negatively.
  4. Chat Function: Only for class-related discussions.
  5. Disciplinary Action: The teacher has the authority to remove or bar students causing disturbances.
